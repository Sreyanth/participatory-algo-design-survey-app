In this study, you will have the opportunity to earn additional money by making accurate predictions about high school students' test performance. Please read all information very carefully.

In this study, you will estimate the performance of 20 high school sophomores on a standardized reading test. The scores range from 1-100. A score of 63 indicates that the student was in the 63rd percentile (63%) of all students for the standardized reading test. This means that the student performed better than 63% of the other students who took the test. The students who did the best are in the 100th percentile. The students who did the worst are in the 1st percentile.
