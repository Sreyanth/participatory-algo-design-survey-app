Understand the Payment Structure

You will make 20 official estimates in a few moments.

{% if user_group.use_model_estimates_only %}

You will be given a choice of having your estimated ranks count as the official estimates or having the model’s estimated ranks count as the official estimates.

{% elif user_group.use_freely %}

You will be able to use the estimates produced by the statistical model to make your official estimates.

{% endif %}

During the official round, you will receive additional bonus money based on the accuracy of the official estimates. You can earn $1 to $5 depending on how close the official estimates are to the actual ranks.

The bonus will be determined as follows:

  • $5 — within 5 percentiles of students’ actual percentiles on average
  • $4 — within 10 percentiles of students’ actual percentiles on average
  • $3 — within 15 percentiles of students’ actual percentiles on average
  • $2 — within 20 percentiles of students’ actual percentiles on average
  • $1 — within 25 percentiles of students’ actual percentiles on average
