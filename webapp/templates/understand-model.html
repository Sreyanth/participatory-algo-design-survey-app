The Statistical Model

In addition to the forecasts that you produce, there are also forecasts of each percentile that were produced by a statistical model. The model is designed to forecast each student’s percentile. The model is based on data from thousands of high school sophomores, and it uses the same information that you will receive. The model does not have any additional information that you will not receive. This is a sophisticated model, put together by the research team.

Across all students, the model’s estimate is off by 19.7 percentiles on average. Although this is the model’s average performance, it may perform better or worse than this for the small set of students that you will be asked about in this experiment.
